About SGMS

SGMS is not just a website, It was a dream of a student to develop something unique for students, So that they can easily share their "Thoughts , Suggestions and Grievances" to the higher Authorities of the Colleges and Universities etc.
How It Works :
Whenever any Student comes to us and Register himself/herself in our website then ultimayely he/she becomes eligible to file any Grievance on different-2 levels (like Department/College/University etc.) After filling the Grievance Form Successfuly ultimately, It is sent to our Grievance Committee members at different-different levels. As you can see in the Navigation bar also.Our Committee members will look on your Grievance. If it will be found genuine then Immediately possilbe legal actions will be taken.
